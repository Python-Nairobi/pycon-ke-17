Something good

For all Py-Kind!

PyConKe will be the first in a series of anual gatherings of software developers, techies, business people, startups, learning institutions, students and other organizations and individuals that use or otherwise have an interest/stake in the Python programming language and its ecosystem.

Topics featuring in the event...

Web, Music & Animation

Pythonic web frameworks, scripting in animation with Blender and coding music in Py with sonicpi. Plenty of talks and tutorials on these to get you started.

Data Science & AI

Stats crunching with Pandas & co. and ML with with tools like Tensorflow. Come partake in some pythonic stats & AI (just don't let what you subsequently create become self aware).

Internet of Things

Python in my IoT? It's more likely than you think! Feauted at the conference will be talks and presentations on (micro)python on assorted hardware with example applications.

  • ... and then some

Pre-Event Kids' tech workshops

It's never too early for kids to get into tech, and for young people to become teachers. This conference isn't just about those of us who are already into tech, but about lighting a spark in the future generation.

In the leadup to the conference, there will be 3 full day kids workshop events, events teaching kids digital era skills featuring.....

Music with Sonicpi ..and some Py ;)

SonicPi (with Python bindings) enables the young ones to learn to code while making music. Our partner trainers have experience setting up and running training programs for kids of all ages in places ranging from Kibera to Turkana, and will even digitize the curricula we develop to spread the learning further.

Robotics & Internet of Things with microPython

Learning to code by controlling real-world objects is exciting and can help develop a passion for computer programming using Python. During the lead up to the conference, we will also be starting them off early into STEM by teaching them how to control robotic arms and tracked vehicles plus other entry level electronics concepts.
