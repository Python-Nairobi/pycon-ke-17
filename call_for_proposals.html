Call for Proposals

The call for proposals is on!

One of the main goals of this PyConKE is to share the knullidge! If you have something pythonic that you would like to share/teach/present at the conference, we are all ears!

"PyCon is for Python enthusiasts of all experience levels, from new users to core developers."- The PSF on PyCon

Your propoposal will never be too entry-level or advanced to feature at this event. Keeping that in mind, go ahead and fill in the volunteer form below and when you do, someone from the commitee will reach out to you.
